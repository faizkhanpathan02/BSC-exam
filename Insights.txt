RETAIL SALES STRATEGY INSIGHTS – Q2 2024 ANALYSIS
====================================================
Dataset: 5,000 Transactions | Jan–Jun 2024 | 4 Regions | 6 Categories

KEY INSIGHTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. EAST REGION DOMINATES TOTAL REVENUE (~30% contribution)
   The East region is the highest-performing geography, contributing nearly
   30% of total company sales. Q3 strategy should double down here with
   targeted campaigns and premium product placements to maximise ROI.

2. ELECTRONICS IS THE SINGLE LARGEST REVENUE CATEGORY
   Electronics (led by Samsung Galaxy S23 and Dell Laptops) generates the
   highest sales value per transaction. High unit prices combined with steady
   volume make this category a strategic priority — inventory should be fully
   stocked ahead of Q3's festive season.

3. ONLINE CHANNEL OUTPERFORMS OFFLINE (55%+ of sales)
   Online transactions account for over 55% of total revenue, signalling a
   clear digital-first preference among customers. Budget reallocation from
   physical store marketing toward digital ads, SEO, and app UX improvements
   will yield compounding returns in Q3 and beyond.

4. ~40+ HIGH-VALUE LOYAL CUSTOMERS IDENTIFIED (10+ PURCHASES EACH)
   A cohort of repeat customers made more than 10 purchases during the
   6-month period. These VIP customers collectively represent a disproportionate
   share of revenue. Launching a tiered loyalty rewards program or exclusive
   early-access offers for this segment will increase retention and LTV.

5. THE WEST REGION IS UNDERPERFORMING — NEEDS MARKETING SUPPORT
   The West region contributes the lowest share of total sales (~20–22%).
   A geo-targeted campaign combining localised offers, regional influencer
   partnerships, and exclusive bundles could bridge the gap with East and
   South in Q3 — representing untapped growth potential.

6. MONTH-OVER-MONTH SALES SHOW A POSITIVE UPWARD TRAJECTORY
   Sales grew consistently from January through June 2024 across most
   categories and regions. Capitalising on this momentum with Q3 seasonal
   offers (Independence Day, back-to-school, festive pre-season) can drive
   a 15–20% incremental uplift over Q2 performance.

7. GROCERIES & BEAUTY ARE HIGH-FREQUENCY, LOW-VALUE CATEGORIES
   While Electronics drives revenue per transaction, Groceries and Beauty
   drive transaction frequency. Cross-selling high-margin accessories and
   combo packs (e.g., Grocery + Kitchen Appliance bundles) represents a
   significant basket-size expansion opportunity that current data confirms
   is underexplored.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RECOMMENDED Q3 ACTIONS
• Focus ad spend on East region + Online channel
• Launch Electronics pre-festive sale in late July
• Activate loyalty program for 10+ purchase customer segment
• Run West region geo-targeted promotional campaign
• Introduce bundle cross-sell between Groceries + Electronics
